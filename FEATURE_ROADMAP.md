### Competitive Analysis Focus

#### Key Differentiators Needed
- **Speed**: Faster quote turnaround than competitors
- **Quality**: Higher verification standards
- **Technology**: Better user experience and tools
- **Support**: Superior customer service
- **Value**: Competitive pricing with better outcomes
- **Trust**: Stronger safety and guarantee programs

#### Market Positioning
- Premium platform for quality-conscious customers
- Professional growth platform for serious tradies
- Technology-forward solution with traditional service values
- Local expertise with national scale
